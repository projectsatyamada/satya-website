Update

We bought land and a building in Doany. Now we need 3,500€ to renovate it.

June 2026 · Arturo

I want to tell you what's actually happened, because it changes things quite a bit.

The village lies near Doany, in the mountains — hard to reach, far from everything. That's exactly why issues like healthcare and education have to be tackled directly on the ground. There's simply no other way to get there.

We found a building in Doany — an old house at the edge of the village that wasn't being used. We were able to buy it, land included, with private funds. That means the dispensary now has a real place. It exists. It just needs to be renovated.

What was originally planned as a 16,500€ new build is now a renovation — 3,500€ for the work and basic medical equipment. That's it. That's what this campaign is about.

Our nurse Lalao is already in Doany. I personally cover most of her salary. She's doing her job with what she has — but she needs a proper space to work in. That's what the dispensary gives her.

If you'd like to help — once, monthly, or as an association member — you can do so via HelloAsso (with official tax receipt), GoFundMe, or direct bank transfer. Everything's on the website. And if you just share this — that counts too. 🙏
